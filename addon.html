How to Install Addons in Minecraft PE

  1. Make sure you have downloaded and installed Minecraft PE on your device. You can download it from the app store that is compatible with your device's operating system.
  2. Find the addon you want to install. You can search for it on websites that provide Minecraft addons. Ensure that the addon you choose is compatible with the version of Minecraft PE you have.
  3. Once you have found the desired addon, download the addon file to your device. The file will have the .mcaddon or .mcpack extension.
  4. After the download is complete, locate the addon file on your device. Usually, the addon file will be in the "Downloads" folder.
  5. Double-tap on the addon file. This will open Minecraft PE and import the addon into the game.
  6. After the addon is imported, you will see a confirmation message in Minecraft PE. Follow the instructions that appear on the screen to enable the addon.
  7. Once the addon is enabled, you can start a new game or continue an existing game with the addon installed.
  8. To use the addon, open the inventory in the game and check the "Resource Packs" or "Behavior Packs" tab. Here, you will find the installed addon. Activate the addon by clicking the checkbox next to it.
  9. Now you have successfully installed an addon in Minecraft PE. Enjoy the new experience in the game!
