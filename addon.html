How to Install Mods and Skins for Minecraft PE

  1. Open the Minecraft PE Mods and Skins application on your device.
  2. Select the mod or skin that you want to install.
  3. Download the mod or skin file to your device, or you can also choose to install it directly through the application.
  4. Once the mod is installed, launch Minecraft Pocket Edition (MCPE).
  5. Enjoy playing Minecraft PE with your newly installed mod or skin!

Thank you for using our guide to learn how to install mods and skins for Minecraft PE.
